### `Optical-Flow` Developed [`glsl-optical-flow`](https://epok.tech/glsl-optical-flow){target="_blank"}, an `Optical-Flow` Computer-Vision method for natural-motion interaction via video – try it with the [camera enabled](.){.demo-camera-on}. This natural-motion interaction embraces myriad kinds of people, forms, movements, abilities, appearances, and environments – intuitively and in real-time. Developed to run optimally on the `GPU` via `GLSL`, and published [Open](https://www.npmjs.com/package/@epok.tech/glsl-optical-flow){target="_blank"}-[Source](https://github.com/keeffEoghan/glsl-optical-flow){target="_blank"}.
