### *Shadows touch across time* [*Shadows touch across time*](https://epok.tech/shadows-touch-across-time-demo){target="_blank"}, an interactive artwork installation commissioned by [Olta](https://olta.art/){target="_blank"} and [Arweave](https://arweave.org/){target="_blank"}, for exhibition at [Arweave Day 2025](https://lu.ma/arweaveday2025){target="_blank"} conference in Berlin. People connect across time through gestures, an emergent collaboration co-creating the artwork.
The fluid flows with your motions, your hand is a print made of solid particles; occasionally trails of other handprints appear, shadows left by those before you – if your hand touches theirs before they dissolve, your handprint is traced into the artwork, another shadow for a future person to touch.
Together, hands connect across the artwork, as you respond to each others' gestures in a discourse, your shadows touch across time. Combines and develops the fluid-dynamics and `AI` hand-tracking elements of **Locus** into a different creative execution – try it with the [camera enabled](.){.demo-camera-on}. Integrates [`AI` hand-tracking](https://mediapipe-studio.webapps.google.com/demo/hand_landmarker){target="_blank"} to capture traces of audience hand gestures and drive this dialogue. Integrates [`gl-mpm`](#prototype-mpm) and [`glsl-optical-flow`](#prototype-optical-flow) for fluid-dynamics simulation and natural-motion interaction via camera to influence it.
