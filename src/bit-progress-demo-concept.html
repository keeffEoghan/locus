### Progress demo ~ concept [Hand-drawn concept art sketches](https://youtu.be/bS0p8n_VcEY){target="_blank"} exploring each aspect of the artwork aesthetic. In this demo we explore the concept layer by layer.
First, the outer bust surface, then the translucent inner anatomy, then blended with fluid particles flowing through and matching anatomical colours, finally the flowing particles alone tracing hints of anatomy forms.
Following these are AI-generated variations, exploring a diversity of potential aesthetics to widen the explored space. This concept emerged from my own experiences with meditation – the process and practice as I experience it, expressed allegorically in a way that evokes similar feelings in me.
I set out from this point with the aim of engaging other people in an experience which helped me establish a resilient foundation and balanced outlook in my life – expressed wordlessly and experientially, directly subjective not prescriptively descriptive. To explore the concept sketch layers, slide on the demo to expand and peel through its layers and variations.
