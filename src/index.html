## *Locus* is an interactive digital art installation, online experience, and `NFT`{.info title="Non-Fungible Token, a cryptocurrency asset"} series. It aims to engage these **direct participatory experiences**; to explore and co-create, find their own insights and reflect. As flowing particles trace layers in a translucent human form, the audience and artwork interact by natural motion, to co-create a meditative experience.
