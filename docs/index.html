Locus is an interactive digital artwork

Aiming to engage audiences in direct participatory experiences evoking meditation, it will be exhibited as a physical installation, free online experience, and NFT series.

As flowing particles trace layers in a translucent human form, the audience and artwork interact by natural motion to co-create a meditative experience.

Aesthetic

Millions of particles pass in fluid motion through a translucent human bust – their colours match the forms they flow within (skin, bone, muscle, organs), tracing ephemeral hints of shapes and colours layered in the depths.

Fluid motion elicits meditative obervation of sensations (bodily, thought, emotional) – both are dynamic, interconnected, chaotic processes.

Interaction

Your natural motions affect the flow – movement agitates it into turbulence, clouding outer surfaces; stillness settles it into smooth filaments, revealing inner depths.

This interaction encourages mindful reflective awareness, in contrast to busy activity – as still water’s clearer than an agitated surface, you see more deeply into the truths within.
