How to Contribute

You can contribute to Locus using crypto or card while Artizen Season 3 is running until late January 2024:

Crypto Payment ~ until 29 January 2024 ~ Ξ0.01+

Your entire contribution goes to the project intact, without covering transfer-fees (gas), and you can receive Artifacts and rewards to your crypto wallet.

  1. If you don’t have a crypto wallet, you can get one via a service like MetaMask (free and recommended).
  2. Check out Locus on Artizen to explore, connect your wallet, and buy Artifacts to support the project!
  3. Each Artifact is Ξ0.01 (about US$23), collect as many as you like to contribute and earn rewards!

This crypto payment option remains open until Season 3 ends on 29 January 2024.

Card Payment ~ until 27 January 2024 ~ US$75+

No crypto required for your contribution, but part of it goes to cover transfer-fees (gas), and you can’t receive Artifacts nor any rewards that need a crypto wallet.

  1. Visit the card payment page and enter the project name along with your details.
  2. Ensure you copy the name correctly as “Locus” to specify “What project do you want to support?”
  3. A US$75 minimum applies to cover fluctuating transfer-fees (gas) along with a share for the project.
  4. Batch or pool Artifacts into one transfer to pay less in fees (gas, applied per transfer, big or small).

Artizen’s community treasury receives any remainder below Ξ0.01 / US$23 along with unsent Artifacts.

Card payment takes up to 48 hours, so this option ends on 27 January 2024, 48h earlier than crypto payment.
